Your Skin Bae Vitamin C 30ml

The Avoskin serum from the Your Skin Bae Series functions as a brightening serum. It contains a blend of brightening ingredients, including Vitamin C, Niacinamide, and Mandarin Orange Extract, which are effective in brightening the skin, fading acne scars, moisturizing the skin, minimizing the appearance of pores, acting as an antioxidant, and promoting healthier-looking skin. Vitamin C works by inhibiting melanin production and accumulation in the skin, making it highly effective for achieving optimal skin brightening.

Your Skin Bae Shield of Sun SPF 50 PA++++ 30ml

The first sunscreen from the Your Skin Bae Series features a lightweight cream texture that absorbs easily. This formula provides protection to help maintain bright, hydrated, and healthy skin. Its key ingredients include Sodium Hyaluronate, Chamomile Flower Extract, and Allantoin, which moisturize and soothe the skin. Combined with 3-O-Ethyl Ascorbic Acid, it is effective in brightening the facial skin.

Your Skin Bae Lactic Acid 30ml

The Avoskin Lactic Acid Serum from the Your Skin Bae Series is a mild exfoliating serum suitable for dry skin. Lactic Acid, known for being gentler compared to other AHAs, is combined with Niacinamide and Kiwi Extract. This serum helps remove dead skin cells, stimulates skin regeneration, brightens the skin, reduces the appearance of fine lines, diminishes pigmentation, including acne scars, and hydrates the skin, making pores appear less visible. Its high antioxidant content ensures the skin is well-nourished and cared for

Your Skin Bae Tranexamic Acid 30ml

One of the serums from the Your Skin Bae Series is an excellent choice for brightening and evening out skin tone. It contains key ingredients such as 3% Tranexamic Acid, Glycolic Acid, Cranberry, and Licorice, which work to fade dark spots, reduce Post-Inflammatory Hyperpigmentation (PIH) and Post-Inflammatory Erythema (PIE), and soothe mildly irritated skin. Tranexamic Acid acts as a gentle brightening agent with anti-inflammatory properties, making it particularly suitable for sensitive skin. Additionally, Glycolic Acid, Cranberry, and Licorice help moisturize, brighten, and maintain the skin barrier.
